GENETIC OPTIMIZATION –Travel Through 

Team member(s): Manoj Nagarajan, Darkhan Kadirov and Miguel Giménez Pérez
Modified by Manoj Nagarajan on May 30, 2023

Manifesto

To design a shaded walkway on a sloped terrain, with hardscapes and softscapes as obstacles. Developing a evolutionary algorithm(Wallacei) to define a shortest path(DG) that adaptes the shape with environmental data(LB) and physics solver(KG2) such that the design is hormonus with the nature(CA).
